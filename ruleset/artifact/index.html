Artifacts

Artifacts are items that store elemental power within them granting the Character additional power. To craft an Artifact, the Character needs the Essences obtained from defeating Enemies. Artifacts can be further powered up by enchanting them with additional properties. Lastly, they can be dismantled back into Essences if the Character decides they do not need the Artifact.

Essence

Essences are a concentration of Element given physical form. These are obtained by defeating Enemies, which determines the Essence’s Element and Grade. The Essence’s Element is dependent on the Enemy’s highest EM value. The Essence’s Grade is dependent on the Enemy’s Level, and requires an Essence Die roll to determine the Grade. See the table below for more information.

Enemy Level Essence Die Obtained Essence Grade by Essence Die Result

4 - 6 1d8 1 - 2: No Essence 3 - 6: Lesser 7 - 8: Normal -

10 1d12 1: No Essence 2 - 5: Lesser 6 - 9: Normal 10 - 12: Greater

Refining

Lower Grade Essences can be refined into higher Grade Essences. Refining requires 4 Essences of the same Element and Grade to be consumed to obtain 1 Essence of the same Element and next Grade above.

Dismantling

Dismantling allows for the Artifacts to be reduced back to Essences. When Dismantling, the Character rolls a Dismantle Die based on the highest Essence Grade used on the Artifact or its Enchantment. The Dismantling Die results determines the Grade of the Essence returned. The Element of the returned Essence is determined by the highest number of same Element Essences used in the Crafting of the Artifact.

Highest Essence Grade Dismantle Die Returned Essence Grade by Dismantle Die Result
Lesser 1d6 1 - 4: No Essence 5 - 6: Lesser - -
Normal 1d8 1 - 4: No Essence 5 - 7: Lesser 8: Normal -
Greater 1d10 1 - 5: No Essence 6 - 7: Lesser 8 - 9: Normal 10: Greater
