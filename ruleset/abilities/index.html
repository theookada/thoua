Ability

Abilities are unique actions that the Character can use in Combat. The Character may purchase a variety of Abilities using their CP. What combination of Abilities purchased by the Character largely decides how the Character plays in Combat.

Element

Each Ability is tied to a specific Element. This Element is used to determine which of the Character’s EMs are raised and lowered and which EM Die the Enemy uses to defend against the Ability Die.

Rank

Rank determines the Elemental strength of the Ability. The Rank is used to determine how much the Character’s EMs are raised and lowered as well as track how attuned the Character is to a specific Element. At Level 1, the Character is able to purchase any Rank 1 Ability. However to purchase higher Rank Abilities, the Character must have purchased at least one Ability that is one Rank lower of the same Element.

CP Cost

CP Cost is how much CP the Character needs to spend in order to purchase an Ability.

Type

Type categorizes Abilities in Damage, Control, Support or Passive based on the Ability’s Effect.

  • Damage: Deals Element Damage to an Enemy.
  • Control: Places Penalties or harmful Conditions on an Enemy.
  • Support: Heals or gives helpful Condition to an Ally.
  • Passive: Grants the Character Bonuses or unique Effects to themselves.

AP Cost

AP Cost is how much AP the Character needs to spend on their Turn to use the Ability in Combat.

Cooldown

Cooldown is the number of Turns the Character must wait before using an Ability again. Cooldowns are reduced by -1 Turn at the End of the Character’s Turn.

Die Roll

Die Roll indicates the specific Attacker Roll used for the Ability.

Target

Target indicates if the Ability affects Allies, Enemies, the Character or an AoE.

  • Ally: The Ability affects the Character and/or the Character’s Ally.
  • Enemy: The Ability affects the Character’s Enemy.
  • Self: The Ability affects only the Character.
  • Target: The Ability can affect Allies, Enemies or Self.
  • AoE: The Ability affects multiple Targets in an area. (See Combat for more)

Range

Range indicates the maximum number of spaces away from the Character the Target can be. For AoEs, Range indicates the number of spaces away from the Character the Ability begins or the maximum number of spaces away the AoE reaches. (See Combat for more)

Effect

Effect is what the Ability does when used in Combat. Abilities have a variety of Effects that are useful in a variety of situations.

Special

Special is either an additional or upgraded Ability Effect that is used if the Character meets the indicated requirements listed.
