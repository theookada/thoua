About

Hi there! My name is Theo Okada or “Riesling” on Discord. Thank you for taking the time to look at THOUA. I grew up playing D&D 3.5 and Pathfinder and enjoyed being able to have a creative outlet that I could share with friends. I also enjoyed playing JPRG such as Final Fantasy Tactics and Fire Emblem for the turn-based grid combat. Additionally, I love MMOs such as Final Fantasy XIV for their raids and community. So I wanted to combine my favorite aspects of those games into a tabletop system that people new to the genre could try and for vetern players to modify and customize.

I hope with THOUA new Players are encouraged to try Tabletops and create experiences and stories that the Players’ and GM can enjoy together.

Support

If you would like to support THOUA, all I ask is for you to give feedback on the system on the Discord, share and play it. If you would like to support THOUA to grow it into something more; please go to Near the Resolution and Donate.
